Popular Dubai Activities

UAE is home to some of the largest, most-popular and awarded theme parks and malls. There are few things no one should miss out on when visiting this country. We provide land packages along with entry tickets, fast track passes, airport transfers, hotel bookings and visas. Our services and packages are not limited and can be customized according to your requirements.

AT THE TOP – BURJ KHALIFA

With 2 observation decks and the world highest lounge, this experience is not to be skipped when In Dubai. The world’s tallest tower has observation decks at the 124th and 148th floor. This at the top experience gives you a bird’s eye view of the city while learning about the past and present of Dubai. We are below mentioned ticket options available during peak and off-peak timing. We also provide fast track entry tickets for the same.
At the Top – Burj Khalifa – 125/124 floor
At the Top – Burj Khalifa Sky – 148th & 124th floor

BURJ AL ARAB

The second most talked about structure in UAE is the Burj Al Arab. The 7 star hotel built on its own island and equipped with the most luxurious suites. If you want to explore underwater dining, drinks at 660ft above the Persian Gulf, the world’s Best Luxury Hotel Spa, or stay at one of the 202 bedroom suites, we can arrange all of this and more. Drop us an email to enquire more.

THE DUBAI MALL

The largest in the world and home to the most luxurious brands, the Dubai Mall is a shopper’s paradise. You can spend an entire day at this mall and still not walk around most of it. The mall includes a gigantic aquarium with an underwater tunnel and zoo, 22 screens cinema, VR Park, 1200 shops, SEGA indoor theme park, KidZania, Olympic sized ice skating rink, Dubai Mall fountain and over 120 cafes and restaurants. Services we provide include and are not limited to below:

  • Pick up and drop off services
  • Dubai Mall Aquarium and Underwater Zoo tickets
  • SEGA World tickets
  • KidZania tickets
  • Dubai Fountain boat ride
